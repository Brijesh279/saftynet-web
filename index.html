Technology Used And
How it Works



Intelligent video surveillance, using algorithms like YOLO, works by analyzing real-time video feeds from cameras. YOLO (You Only Look Once) is an efficient object detection algorithm that quickly identifies and tracks objects in the video stream, such as people, vehicles, and other items of interest. The COCO dataset, on the other hand, is a large collection of images with annotations that serves as a benchmark for training and evaluating the performance of object detection algorithms like YOLO, ensuring their accuracy and effectiveness in real-world scenarios.
